Our Story

Artistry & Passion

Nanté Patisserie is more than a cake studio. It is the culmination of a lifelong dream, built on a foundation of classic technique and contemporary artistry.

Meet Our Founder

Chef Eléonore Nanté

Trained at the prestigious Le Cordon Bleu in Paris, Chef Nanté spent over a decade honing her craft in Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe before founding her own studio.

Her philosophy is simple: use the finest ingredients, respect the process, and never forget that a cake is not just dessert—it is the centerpiece of a memory. Her work has been featured in Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, and The Knot as a leading voice in modern cake design.
